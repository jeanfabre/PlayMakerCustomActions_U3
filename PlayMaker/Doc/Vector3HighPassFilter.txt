<div id="actionDesc">
<p>
Use a high pass filter to isolate sudden changes in a Vector3 Variable. Useful when working with Get Device Acceleration to remove the constant effect of gravity.</p>
</div>
<div id="paramRow">
	<div id="paramName">
Vector 3Variable</div>
	<div id="paramDesc">
Vector3 Variable to filter. Should generally come from some constantly updated input, e.g., acceleration.</div>
</div>

<div id="paramRow">
	<div id="paramName">
Filtering Factor</div>
	<div id="paramDesc">
Determines how much influence new changes have.</div>
</div>
