<p>
Blends an Animation towards a Target Weight over a specified Time.
Optionally sends an Event when finished.</p>
</div>

	<div id="paramName">
Game Object</div>
	<div id="paramDesc">
The GameObject to animate.</div>
</div>

<div id="paramRow">
	<div id="paramName">
Anim Name</div>
	<div id="paramDesc">
The name of the animation to blend.</div>
</div>

<div id="paramRow">
	<div id="paramName">
Target Weight</div>
	<div id="paramDesc">
Target weight to blend to.</div>
</div>

<div id="paramRow">
	<div id="paramName">
Time</div>
	<div id="paramDesc">
How long should the blend take.</div>
</div>

<div id="paramRow">
	<div id="paramName">
Finish Event</div>
	<div id="paramDesc">
Event to send when the blend has finished.</div>
</div>
