<div id="actionDesc">
<p>
Enables/Disables an Animation on a GameObject.
Animation time is paused while disabled. Animation must also have a non zero weight to play.</p>
</div>
<div id="paramRow">
	<div id="paramName">
Game Object</div>
	<div id="paramDesc">
The GameObject playing the animation.</div>
</div>

<div id="paramRow">
	<div id="paramName">
Anim Name</div>
	<div id="paramDesc">
The name of the animation to enable/disable.</div>
</div>

<div id="paramRow">
	<div id="paramName">
Enable</div>
	<div id="paramDesc">
Set to True to enable, False to disable.</div>
</div>

<div id="paramRow">
	<div id="paramName">
Reset On Exit</div>
	<div id="paramDesc">
Reset the initial enabled state when exiting the state.</div>
</div>
