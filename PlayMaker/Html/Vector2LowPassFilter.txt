<div id="actionDesc">
<p>
Use a low pass filter to reduce the influence of sudden changes in a Vector2 Variable.</p>
</div>
<div id="paramRow">
	<div id="paramName">
Vector 2Variable</div>
	<div id="paramDesc">
Vector2 Variable to filter. Should generally come from some constantly updated input</div>
</div>

<div id="paramRow">
	<div id="paramName">
Filtering Factor</div>
	<div id="paramDesc">
Determines how much influence new changes have. E.g., 0.1 keeps 10 percent of the unfiltered vector and 90 percent of the previously filtered value</div>
</div>
