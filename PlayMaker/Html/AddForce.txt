<div id="actionDesc">
<p>
Adds a force to a Game Object. Use Vector3 variable and/or Float variables for each axis.</p>
</div>
<div id="paramRow">
	<div id="paramName">
Game Object</div>
	<div id="paramDesc">
The GameObject to apply the force to.</div>
</div>

<div id="paramRow">
	<div id="paramName">
At Position</div>
	<div id="paramDesc">
Optionally apply the force at a position on the object. This will also add some torque. The position is often returned from MousePick or GetCollisionInfo actions.</div>
</div>

<div id="paramRow">
	<div id="paramName">
Vector</div>
	<div id="paramDesc">
A Vector3 force to add. Optionally override any axis with the X, Y, Z parameters.</div>
</div>

<div id="paramRow">
	<div id="paramName">
X</div>
	<div id="paramDesc">
Force along the X axis. To leave unchanged, set to 'None'.</div>
</div>

<div id="paramRow">
	<div id="paramName">
Y</div>
	<div id="paramDesc">
Force along the Y axis. To leave unchanged, set to 'None'.</div>
</div>

<div id="paramRow">
	<div id="paramName">
Z</div>
	<div id="paramDesc">
Force along the Z axis. To leave unchanged, set to 'None'.</div>
</div>

<div id="paramRow">
	<div id="paramName">
Space</div>
	<div id="paramDesc">
Apply the force in world or local space.</div>
</div>

<div id="paramRow">
	<div id="paramName">
Force Mode</div>
	<div id="paramDesc">
The type of force to apply. See Unity Physics docs.</div>
</div>

<div id="paramRow">
	<div id="paramName">
Every Frame</div>
	<div id="paramDesc">
Repeat every frame while the state is active.</div>
</div>
