<div id="actionDesc">
<p>
Linearly interpolates between 2 vectors.</p>
</div>
<div id="paramRow">
	<div id="paramName">
From Vector</div>
	<div id="paramDesc">
First Vector.</div>
</div>

<div id="paramRow">
	<div id="paramName">
To Vector</div>
	<div id="paramDesc">
Second Vector.</div>
</div>

<div id="paramRow">
	<div id="paramName">
Amount</div>
	<div id="paramDesc">
Interpolate between From Vector and ToVector by this amount. Value is clamped to 0-1 range. 0 = From Vector; 1 = To Vector; 0.5 = half way between.</div>
</div>

<div id="paramRow">
	<div id="paramName">
Store Result</div>
	<div id="paramDesc">
Store the result in this vector variable.</div>
</div>

<div id="paramRow">
	<div id="paramName">
Every Frame</div>
	<div id="paramDesc">
Repeat every frame. Useful if any of the values are changing.</div>
</div>
