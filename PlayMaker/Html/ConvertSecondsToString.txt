<div id="actionDesc">
<p>
Converts Seconds to a String value representing the time.</p>
</div>
<div id="paramRow">
	<div id="paramName">
Seconds Variable</div>
	<div id="paramDesc">
The seconds variable to convert.</div>
</div>

<div id="paramRow">
	<div id="paramName">
String Variable</div>
	<div id="paramDesc">
A string variable to store the time value.</div>
</div>

<div id="paramRow">
	<div id="paramName">
Format</div>
	<div id="paramDesc">
Format. 0 for days, 1 is for hours, 2 for minutes, 3 for seconds and 4 for milliseconds. 5 for total days, 6 for total hours, 7 for total minutes, 8 for total seconds and 9 for total milliseconds. so {2:D2} would just show the seconds of the current time, NOT the grand total number of seconds, the grand total of seconds would be {8:F0}</div>
</div>

<div id="paramRow">
	<div id="paramName">
Every Frame</div>
	<div id="paramDesc">
Repeat every frame. Useful if the seconds variable is changing.</div>
</div>
