<div id="actionDesc">
<p>
Transforms position from world space into screen space. NOTE: Uses the MainCamera!</p>
</div>
<div id="paramRow">
	<div id="paramName">
World Position</div>
	<div id="paramDesc">
World position to transform into screen coordinates.</div>
</div>

<div id="paramRow">
	<div id="paramName">
World X</div>
	<div id="paramDesc">
World X position.</div>
</div>

<div id="paramRow">
	<div id="paramName">
World Y</div>
	<div id="paramDesc">
World Y position.</div>
</div>

<div id="paramRow">
	<div id="paramName">
World Z</div>
	<div id="paramDesc">
World Z position.</div>
</div>

<div id="paramRow">
	<div id="paramName">
Store Screen Point</div>
	<div id="paramDesc">
Store the screen position in a Vector3 Variable. Z will equal zero.</div>
</div>

<div id="paramRow">
	<div id="paramName">
Store Screen X</div>
	<div id="paramDesc">
Store the screen X position in a Float Variable.</div>
</div>

<div id="paramRow">
	<div id="paramName">
Store Screen Y</div>
	<div id="paramDesc">
Store the screen Y position in a Float Variable.</div>
</div>

<div id="paramRow">
	<div id="paramName">
Normalize</div>
	<div id="paramDesc">
Normalize screen coordinates (0-1). Otherwise coordinates are in pixels.</div>
</div>

<div id="paramRow">
	<div id="paramName">
Every Frame</div>
	<div id="paramDesc">
Repeat every frame</div>
</div>
