<div id="actionDesc">
<p>
Modify various character controller settings.
'None' leaves the setting unchanged.</p>
</div>
<div id="paramRow">
	<div id="paramName">
Game Object</div>
	<div id="paramDesc">
The GameObject that owns the CharacterController.</div>
</div>

<div id="paramRow">
	<div id="paramName">
Height</div>
	<div id="paramDesc">
The height of the character's capsule.</div>
</div>

<div id="paramRow">
	<div id="paramName">
Radius</div>
	<div id="paramDesc">
The radius of the character's capsule.</div>
</div>

<div id="paramRow">
	<div id="paramName">
Slope Limit</div>
	<div id="paramDesc">
The character controllers slope limit in degrees.</div>
</div>

<div id="paramRow">
	<div id="paramName">
Step Offset</div>
	<div id="paramDesc">
The character controllers step offset in meters.</div>
</div>

<div id="paramRow">
	<div id="paramName">
Center</div>
	<div id="paramDesc">
The center of the character's capsule relative to the transform's position</div>
</div>

<div id="paramRow">
	<div id="paramName">
Detect Collisions</div>
	<div id="paramDesc">
Should other rigidbodies or character controllers collide with this character controller (By default always enabled).</div>
</div>

<div id="paramRow">
	<div id="paramName">
Every Frame</div>
	<div id="paramDesc">
Repeat every frame while the state is active.</div>
</div>
