<div id="actionDesc">
<p>
Applies an explosion Force to all Game Objects with a Rigid Body inside a Radius.</p>
</div>
<div id="paramRow">
	<div id="paramName">
Center</div>
	<div id="paramDesc">
The world position of the center of the explosion.</div>
</div>

<div id="paramRow">
	<div id="paramName">
Force</div>
	<div id="paramDesc">
The strength of the explosion.</div>
</div>

<div id="paramRow">
	<div id="paramName">
Radius</div>
	<div id="paramDesc">
The radius of the explosion. Force falls of linearly with distance.</div>
</div>

<div id="paramRow">
	<div id="paramName">
Upwards Modifier</div>
	<div id="paramDesc">
Applies the force as if it was applied from beneath the object. This is useful since explosions that throw things up instead of pushing things to the side look cooler. A value of 2 will apply a force as if it is applied from 2 meters below while not changing the actual explosion position.</div>
</div>

<div id="paramRow">
	<div id="paramName">
Force Mode</div>
	<div id="paramDesc">
The type of force to apply.</div>
</div>

<div id="paramRow">
	<div id="paramName">
Layer</div>
	<div id="paramDesc">
TODO</div>
</div>

<div id="paramRow">
	<div id="paramName">
Layer Mask</div>
	<div id="paramDesc">
Layers to effect.</div>
</div>

<div id="paramRow">
	<div id="paramName">
Invert Mask</div>
	<div id="paramDesc">
Invert the mask, so you effect all layers except those defined above.</div>
</div>

<div id="paramRow">
	<div id="paramName">
Every Frame</div>
	<div id="paramDesc">
Repeat every frame while the state is active.</div>
</div>
